★ Our story

We're Jayleen & Ashley.

Two women who spent years behind both the corporate boardroom table AND the bar — and realized nobody was doing this the way it deserved to be done.

Your brand deserves to be on the table. Not a tote bag. Not a pen. A drink — with your logo, your colors, your vibe — handed directly to your guests.

You're the creative genius. We're just the vehicle to deliver it. You choose the can design, the flavors, the aesthetic. We show up, we pour, we make your event the one everyone's still talking about.

Girl, let's wow them.
